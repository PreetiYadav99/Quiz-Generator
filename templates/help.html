How to Use Quiz Generator

  • Step 1: Go to the Start Quiz page.
  • Step 2: Enter a topic, paste your notes, or upload a PDF to generate quizzes.
  • Step 3: Select the number of questions you want in the quiz.
  • Step 4: Click on "Generate Quiz" to create a personalized quiz.
  • Step 5: You can view the generated quiz on the page or download it as a PDF.

FAQs

  • What types of quizzes can I create? Multiple-choice questions with four options, one correct.
  • How can I download the quiz? Use the "Download Quiz" button after generating a quiz.
  • Can I upload a PDF? Yes, simply upload a PDF file with your content to generate quizzes.
  • Is this free? Yes, the application is completely free to use!
